Pay another way

You can pay your penalty by bank transfer.

Use the correct bank details for your penalty. There are different bank details depending on whether your penalty reference starts with A, P or U.

! Warning If you’ve paid by bank transfer before, your bank may automatically use the same bank account and it might not be correct. Check you’re paying to the correct bank account for your penalty reference.

Payments by cheque

You can pay by cheque through the post. Make the cheque payable to ‘Companies House only’ and send it to Companies House, with the slip on your penalty notice (‘remittance advice’).
