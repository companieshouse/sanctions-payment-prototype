Pay a penalty to Companies House

Use this service to make an online payment for a penalty issued by Companies House.

What you can do in this service

You can use this service if you or your company has one penalty to pay.

You can pay:

  • a late filing penalty
  • another penalty that has been issued to a company or person

You cannot use this service if you:

  • have more than one penalty to pay for the same company or person
  • want to pay in instalments
  • have already paid part of the penalty
  • have additional court costs or debt collection costs
  • need to pay a large amount over £X

If any of these apply to you, find out other ways you can pay your penalty.

What you'll need

To pay your penalty, you’ll need:

  • a Companies House account - we'll ask you to sign in or create one
  • your penalty reference number
  • the company number or personal reference number
  • a credit or debit card
Where to find your reference numbers
Your reference numbers are shown at the bottom of your penalty notice. [image of letter]

It usually takes around 5 minutes to pay.
