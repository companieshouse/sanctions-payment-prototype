Pay a penalty to Companies House

Use this service to make an online payment by credit or debit card if you've received a penalty notice from Companies House.

You'll need:

  • to sign in to or create a Companies House account
  • a credit or debit card
  • the information on your penalty notice that we sent you in the post

You must pay the full amount.

It usually takes around 5 minutes to pay.

I want to appeal a penalty

Late filing penalties only

You can appeal a late filing penalty (for filing accounts late) through Companies House. You must show that the reasons are exceptional.

Other types of penalties

Read the guidance about the appeals process for other penalties.
Other ways to pay

You can pay your penalty by Bacs bank transfer. This payment method usually takes longer to process (around 3 working days). We also accept overseas bank transfers.

Find out which account details to use for a bank transfer.

You can also find these bank account details on your penalty notice. ​
