Pay late filing accounts penalty via bank transfer

You've told us that your penalty reference starts with A. Use these account details to pay this penalty.

You can also pay by:

  • Bacs
  • cheque

You'll need to know your company name and number, and the amount to be paid. You'll find your company number on your penalty notice.

Pay by Bacs

You can pay using a Bacs payment.

It usually takes up to 3 working days for your payment to reach Companies House. Check your bank's transaction limits and processing times before making a payment.

You'll need to use the Companies House sort code, account number and account name.

  • Sort code - 60 70 80
  • Account number - 10031154
  • Account name - COMPANIES HOUSE

If you're paying from an overseas account

You can pay through a bank transfer from an overseas account.

You'll need to use the Companies House swift code, account number and account name.

  • Account number (IBAN) - GB79NWBK60708010031154
  • Swift code - NW BK GB 2L
  • Account name - COMPANIES HOUSE

Email the LFP finance team at Companies House to tell them you’ve paid. Include in your email the:

  • company number
  • company name
  • penalty reference number
  • amount paid
  • date paid

Pay by cheque

You can pay by cheque through the post. Make the cheque payable to ‘Companies House only’ and send it along with the slip on the penalty notice (‘remittance advice’) to Companies House.
