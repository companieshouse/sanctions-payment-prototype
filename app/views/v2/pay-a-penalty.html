Pay a penalty to Companies House

Use this service to make an online payment if you’ve received a penalty notice from Companies House.

You'll need:

  • the penalty reference number
  • another number from your penalty notice
Where to find these details

You'll find these numbers at the top of the penalty notice Companies House sent to you.



The first number is called a penalty reference number. This starts with a letter, followed by 7 numbers. For example, A1234567.

The second number might be called a case ID or sanction ID, or it might be your company number. This depends on what type of penalty you are paying. We will tell you what you need to enter.

You'll also need:

  • a Companies House account - we'll ask you to sign in or create one
  • a credit or debit card

It usually takes around 5 minutes to pay.

Start now

Appeal a penalty

You can appeal a late filing penalty through Companies House, but it will only be successful if you can show that the reasons are exceptional.

Other penalties can only be appealed through the courts. You need to tell us if you have appealed to the courts.

Other ways to pay

You can also pay by:

  • Bacs
  • cheque (late filing penalties only)

You'll need the details from your penalty notice, and the amount to be paid.

Pay by Bacs

You can pay using Bacs payment.

It usually takes up to 3 working days for your payment to reach Companies House. Check your bank's transaction limits and processing times before making a payment.

You'll need to use the Companies House sort code, account number and account name.

  • Sort code - 60 70 80
  • Account number - 10031154
  • Account name - COMPANIES HOUSE

If you're paying from an overseas account

You can pay through a bank transfer from an overseas account.

You'll need to use the Companies House swift code, account number and account name.

  • Account number (IBAN) - GB79NWBK60708010031154
  • Swift code - NW BK GB 2L
  • Account name - COMPANIES HOUSE

Email the finance team at Companies House to tell them you’ve paid. Include in your email the:

  • company number
  • company name
  • penalty reference number
  • amount paid
  • date paid

Pay by cheque (late filing penalties only)

You can pay a late filing penalty by cheque, through the post. Make the cheque payable to ‘Companies House only’ and send it along with the slip on the penalty notice (‘remittance advice’) to Companies House.

CF14 3UZ

Write the company name and number on the back of the cheque. You’ll find the company number on your penalty notice.
